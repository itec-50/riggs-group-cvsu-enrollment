CAMPUS HISTORY Cavite State University (CvSU) Bacoor City Campus was established in 2008 to cater to the growing demand for quality higher education in the city of Bacoor and neighboring areas. From humble beginnings using public school facilities, the campus has consistently expanded its program offerings in technology, business, criminology, and social sciences, producing highly skilled professional "Stingrays" who soar globally.

VISION The Premier University in historic Cavite globally recognized for excellence in character development, academics, research, innovation and sustainable community engagement.

MISSION Cavite State University shall provide excellent, equitable and relevant educational opportunities in the arts, sciences and technology through quality instruction and responsive research.

CORE VALUES Truth: Demonstrated by adherence to high ethical standards and commitment to factual accuracy.
Integrity: Upright character, honesty, and consistency in personal and professional behavior.
Excellence: Uncompromising dedication to achieving quality results in all institutional tasks.
Service: Active engagement and responsiveness to the welfare and needs of communities.
