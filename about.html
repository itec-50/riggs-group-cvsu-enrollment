CAMPUS HISTORY Founded by American Thomasites in 1906, Cavite State University (CvSU) started as the Indang Intermediate School. It evolved from a rural high school into Don Severino Agricultural College (DSAC) in 1964. In 1998, it achieved university status. Today, CvSU operates 11 campuses across Cavite, with its 70-hectare main campus located in Indang

VISION The Premier University in historic Cavite globally recognized for excellence in character development, academics, research, innovation and sustainable community engagement.

MISSION Cavite State University shall provide excellent, equitable and relevant educational opportunities in the arts, sciences and technology through quality instruction and responsive research.

CORE VALUES Truth: Objectivity, honesty, and transparency in all academic, research, and personal endeavors.
Integrity: Upholding high ethical standards, honoring public trust, and acting with moral consistency.
Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in academic standards, research, and individual capabilities.
Service: Dedication to public welfare, fairness, community cooperation, and environmental stewardship.
